Hi! I'm Kerit, the creator of Alassie & Bequi Dog Nutrition.

My interest in canine nutrition began through my own dog. When I got my first dog, Alassie, she never seemed particularly enthusiastic about regular kibble. Like many dog owners, I started exploring different feeding options, but at the time I knew very little about raw feeding. When I eventually discovered it, she absolutely loved it. However, raw food was still relatively new in Estonia, and nutritionally balanced options were not always easy to find, so it wasn't the ideal solution for her. That experience sparked my interest in canine nutrition. I wanted to understand what dogs truly need to thrive and how nutrition influences their health, wellbeing, and quality of life. Over time, I developed a vision of creating dog food that would be rich in high-quality animal ingredients, contain only small amounts of plant ingredients, and still be convenient for owners to feed. This passion eventually led me to study food science. While my studies focused primarily on human nutrition, my curiosity about canine nutrition continued to grow. I spent countless hours reading, researching, and learning more about how nutrition affects dogs throughout every stage of life. What ultimately convinced me was Alassie herself. For the first time, she genuinely loved her food, and I could see the difference. Her digestion improved, her coat became softer, and she simply seemed healthier and happier overall. Seeing those changes reinforced my belief that proper nutrition can have a profound impact on a dog's life. Today, I created Alassie & Bequi Dog Nutrition to share practical, evidence-based information and help dog owners make informed decisions about feeding their dogs. My goal is to make canine nutrition easier to understand—without myths, unnecessary complexity, or marketing hype.
